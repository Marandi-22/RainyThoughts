## Boss Progression Requirements

**Acts 1-2:** Small fries (60-85 HP) - Basic stat requirements
**Act 3:** Personal tormentors (90-130 HP) - Moderate stats + personal growth
**Act 4:** Psychological destroyers (140-220 HP) - High stats + streak consistency  
**Act 5:** Inner demon lords (280-450 HP) - Near-perfect stats + long-term commitment
**Final:** Ultimate boss (1000 HP) - Mastery of all aspects + sustained excellence

Each enemy's data triggers determine which of your personal problems, goals, and failures they'll use against you in their AI-generated taunts. The stronger you get, the more afraid they become - except for the always_arrogant ones who never show fear but get progressively more desperate in their dismissals.
